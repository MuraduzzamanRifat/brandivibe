# Brandivibe — Positioning (April Dunford method)

## Step 1: Competitive Alternatives

What would our ideal customer do if Brandivibe didn't exist?

| Tier | Alternative | Who picks this | Why we beat them |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** | basement.studio | Founders who want editorial/typographic premium and have $80-200K | We're cheaper ($35-90K), faster turnaround (6 wks vs. 12), real 3D not just motion |
| **Tier 1** | studio freight | Same — premium editorial with smooth-scroll | We have more WebGL depth. They're minimalist-forward, we span editorial → cinematic → minimal |
| **Tier 1** | lusion.co / Active Theory / Resn | Enterprise brands with $250K-$2M | They're 5-20x our price. We're for founders who want that look but can't justify the spend |
| **Tier 2** | Upwork / Fiverr / freelance Webflow designer | Budget-conscious founders at $2-10K | We give them a custom-coded Next.js site with real 3D and premium brand aesthetic — they give a polished template |
| **Tier 2** | Framer template + tweaks | Designer founders who DIY at $0-5K | We deliver a codebase they own. Framer locks them into a platform and a templatey look |
| **Tier 3** | Webflow in-house (hire a designer) | Series A companies with budget for a FT hire | A designer salary is $140K+/yr. One Brandivibe project at $50K replaces the first 4-5 months and leaves a launched site |
| **Tier 4** | Do nothing — stay on old site | Founders in denial that brand matters | Our battle is against the status quo, not a competitor. We lose deals to inaction more than to other studios |

## Step 2: Unique Attributes

What does Brandivibe have that alternatives don't?

| Attribute | What it means | Proof |
|---|---|---|
| **8-demo range across 8 high-paying verticals** | We can show a client in their industry, in their aesthetic, that we've solved their exact brand problem. Most studios have 3-5 work samples, not 8. | `/helix /neuron /axiom /pulse /aurora /orbit /monolith /atrium` on brandivibe.com |
| **Real WebGL on every project, not stock motion** | Every hero has a custom R3F scene, not a Framer template or Lottie animation. Scroll-linked 3D, custom shaders, environment reflections. | Live demos with 3D that respond to scroll, accessible to `prefers-reduced-motion` |
| **Production-grade Next.js 16 App Router code** | Client owns the code. Server components, lazy-loaded videos, IntersectionObserver, error boundaries, 44px touch targets, metadata + OG cards, zero-overflow responsive. | Public GitHub: `github.com/MuraduzzamanRifat/brandivibe` |
| **Six-week fixed-scope delivery** | No hourly billing, no scope creep. Discovery → design → build → launch in 6 weeks. Agency-standard is 12-16. | Documented process on the site |
| **Poster / print artifact deliverable** | Every demo ships with a museum-quality manifesto poster PNG that the client can literally print and hang. No other studio does this. | `/poster` route, `/helix/poster`, etc. |

## Step 4: Best-fit customer (pulls most value)

From the ICP doc: **Seed to Series B crypto/SaaS/fintech/luxury founder, $1M-$30M raised, has a launch/event in the next 60-90 days, taste-sensitive, has referenced basement.studio / linear / vercel / stripe as brands they admire.**

These customers pull maximum value because:
- They have the **budget** ($35-90K is meaningful but not irrational)
- They have the **taste** to recognize the difference between Brandivibe and a Webflow template
- They have the **urgency** (launch/event) that makes speed matter
- They have the **social graph** (VC Twitter, Product Hunt, TechCrunch) that makes the site a multiplier

## Step 5: Market category

**We compete in**: "Premium independent web design studios"

**Where we stand in that category**: The **affordable premium tier**.

```
   Price →
   $250K ────── $5M           ←  Active Theory / Resn / Huge / Code & Theory
   $80K  ────── $200K         ←  basement.studio / Studio Freight / Exo Ape
   $35K  ────── $90K          ←  BRANDIVIBE  ⭐
   $5K   ────── $25K          ←  Upwork / Fiverr senior freelancers
   $0    ────── $3K           ←  Framer / Webflow templates
```

Our sales pitch: "You wanted Active Theory but can't justify $500K. You tried Webflow but it looks like every other YC startup. We're the bridge — Active Theory-tier craft at an indie studio's price, shipped in 6 weeks."
