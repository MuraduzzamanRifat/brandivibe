# Brandivibe — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Tier A — Primary ICP (target 80% of outbound effort)

### Firmographics
| Dimension | Value |
|---|---|
| **Stage** | Seed to Series B |
| **Funding** | $1M–$30M raised in last 12 months |
| **Revenue** | $0–$15M ARR (often pre-revenue for crypto/launches) |
| **Team size** | 8–80 employees |
| **Industry** | Crypto/DeFi · B2B SaaS · Fintech · AI platforms · Luxury DTC · Luxury EV · Architecture studios · Venture capital firms |
| **Geography** | US (60%), UK (15%), Singapore/HK (10%), Western Europe (15%) |
| **Funding sources** | Top-tier VCs (a16z, Sequoia, Paradigm, USV, Lightspeed, Accel, Kleiner, Benchmark) |

### Psychographics
- **Founder type**: Second-time founder, or first-time founder with design sensibility (ex-designer, ex-Apple, ex-Figma, ex-Stripe)
- **Taste level**: Follows basement.studio / studiofreight / vercel / linear on Twitter. Knows who Active Theory is.
- **Budget mental model**: Thinks "a brand site at $35-90K is a smart investment" rather than "$5K is what websites should cost"
- **Time pressure**: Either launching in 60 days (public launch, token generation event, product hunt, demo day) OR just closed a round and needs to "show up differently"
- **Pain level**: 7-9/10 — "we look like every other YC startup on Webflow"

### Technographics
- Currently on: Webflow template · Framer · Cal.com · Notion · Linear · GitHub · Vercel · Stripe
- Willing to invest in: Custom Next.js, real WebGL, custom CMS
- NOT a fit: WordPress maintenance shops, Shopify-only merchants, companies needing 100+ pages

### Trigger events (buying signals)
1. **Announced a funding round** in the last 90 days (TechCrunch, Crunchbase, The Information)
2. **Launching a new product** in the next 60-90 days (Product Hunt, blog post, Twitter thread)
3. **Token generation event (TGE)** for crypto projects — 30 days out
4. **Founder posted on Twitter/X** "our website looks outdated" or "we need a brand refresh"
5. **Hired a Head of Design / VP Marketing** in the last 60 days
6. **Job posting** for "Senior Brand Designer" — means they can't find one, might outsource
7. **Series A/B company** still on their seed-era landing page (visual audit)

## Tier B — Secondary ICP (target 15% of outbound)

### Firmographics
| Dimension | Value |
|---|---|
| **Stage** | Late Series B to Series C |
| **Revenue** | $15M–$50M ARR |
| **Team size** | 80–200 |
| **Industry** | Enterprise SaaS with active growth motion |

Why secondary: longer sales cycles, more stakeholders, more likely to have in-house design team.

---

## Tier C — Niche ICP (target 5% of outbound)

### Firmographics
- Architecture studios (≥10 people, international clients)
- Boutique luxury brands (jewelry, watches, fragrance) with ≥$5M revenue
- Venture capital firms launching a new fund or rebrand
- Boutique hotels / premium resorts

Why niche: Small market, but extremely high willingness to pay and long-tail referrals.

## ICP Validation Checklist

Every discovery call, score the prospect A/B/C/D:

### Green flags (+1 each, 5+ = Tier A)
- [ ] Funded in the last 12 months
- [ ] Founder/CEO mentions competitor studios by name (basement, lusion, active theory)
- [ ] Has a clear launch/event date driving urgency
- [ ] Budget conversation happens in first call (not dodged)
- [ ] Wants to see the work first before discussing price
- [ ] Mentions ONE specific person on their team who cares about design
- [ ] Has a current website that visibly underserves the brand
- [ ] Mentions a specific Brandivibe demo that resonated ("we want something like Helix")

### Red flags (disqualify if 2+)
- [ ] Asks "how much for a simple 5-page website"
- [ ] Compares us to Fiverr, Upwork freelancers, "my cousin who does websites"
- [ ] Wants unlimited revisions / hourly billing
- [ ] Needs 50+ pages or full e-commerce catalog (not our specialty)
- [ ] Timeline under 3 weeks OR over 6 months
- [ ] Never mentions design quality, only "getting it done"
- [ ] Refuses to share their current site
- [ ] Budget conversation results in "$3-5K is all we can spend"

