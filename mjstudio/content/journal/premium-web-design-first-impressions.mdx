## The Startup Credibility Dilemma

In the startup ecosystem, you're judged by your cover before you even utter a word. The moment a potential investor or customer lands on your site, they're assessing your credibility. A Nielsen Norman Group study reveals that users form an opinion about a website in just 50 milliseconds. If your site fails to hit the right notes, you're potentially kissing goodbye to your funding prospects.

Your website is often the first interaction a potential investor or customer has with your brand.
