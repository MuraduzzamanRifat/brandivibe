## Process
— Process · 005. From kickoff to launch, in six weeks.

01. Discovery. Week 0. 30-min call to understand your brand, goals, and audience. You leave with a crystal-clear brief and a fixed-price proposal within 48h.

02. Design. Week 1-2. High-fidelity Figma mockups in 7-10 days. Two rounds of revisions included. No wireframes — we jump straight to pixel-perfect.

03. Build. Week 3-5. Next.js + TypeScript + WebGL. Weekly Loom updates, staging environment from day one.

04. Launch. Week 6. Performance-tuned (95+ Lighthouse), SEO-audited, CMS-ready. 30 days of post-launch support included.
